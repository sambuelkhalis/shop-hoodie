About Our Hoodies Fabric

The Best Fabric Hoodies Anywhere...

Fleece Fabric

Super soft and lovely to wear, fleece is the modern alternative to wool.Fleece is a type of knit fabric, so its construction can mean it’s more breathable than other synthetic materials. Air can escape through the tiny holes. Made from polyester fiber Fleece is a type of knit fabric, so its construction can mean it’s more breathable.

Sweatshirt Knit Fabric

sweatshirt knit fabric is designed to make sweatshirts. so this fabric is an ideal choice. A sweatshirt knit has a brushed surface and knitted appearance on the reverse. Easy to sew and care for, this fabric is both warm and breathable. so any hoodie made from sweatshirt knit will be more roomy and loose-fitting.

French Terry Fabric

French Terry also known as cloth terry toweling, is a soft and comfortable fabric. made from 100% cotton and is a member of the knit family of textiles. the softer pile side is on the inside of the garment creating a plush, soft texture next to the body. This fabric is incredibly popular for making loungewear.

Jersey Knit Fabric

Jersey Knit fabric has more stretch than a sweatshirt knit. It’s the extra stretch that makes it a great choice for hoodies that need a little more give. This fabric will move with you and won’t feel restrictive on your long hike or jog around the park. It can be made from cotton fiber or a cotton and spandex blend for additional stretch.

Rayon Fabric

As a semi-synthetic fiber, rayon has it all. Lightness, breathability, and the ability to absorb moisture from its natural side. Resistance to mold and bugs from its synthetic half. Rayon isn’t just a good match for wool. It also plays nicely with its synthetic cousins. A spandex and rayon blend will give you an extra stretchy.

Wool Fabric

Wool is still an excellent fabric for hoodies. Soft and strong, wool fibers are incredibly durable and yet extremely light. Wool is an often-overlooked wonder fabric with a built-in resilience to fire, water, and general wear and tear. Most wools used for hoodies are blended with another fiber to counteract the less than attractive.

Durability

Hoodies tend to get a lot of use. Simply because they are so comfortable and soft to wear. Because of this, you need to make sure the fabric is durable enough to survive being worn. Being able to withstand numerous washes in a washing machine and bashed against other clothing in a closet.

100% Cotton Fabric

When you have a hoodie made from 100% cotton, it’s going to be kind to the skin, lightweight, and has an excellent drape. We’ve already looked at three types of fabric made from 100% cotton. Sweatshirt knits, jersey knits, and French terry. A material made from cotton with no other additives like polyester is the best choice.

Comfort Factor

Whatever you want to wear your hoodie for, one thing is an essential must-have. The fabric you choose has to be comfortable. It should also allow freedom of movement. Hoodies aren’t meant to be restrictive or structured. A hoodie isn’t a garment for attending a board-level business meeting.
